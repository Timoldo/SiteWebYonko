Virus

An extremely dangerous virus is here. Ready to kill you. It names : "C6P-08" Discovered in 1896, it has already transforms millions of person into dangerous zombies.

Story

The scenario is inspired by the pandemic suffered around 2019, it is a scenario extrapolated by us to imagine an apocalyptic scenario in which the spread of the virus would be more uncontrollable. Therefore, the idea of putting our characters in a hospital was much more consistent with the subject. The same goes for the resistance base at the Pentagon. The origin of the virus is extravagant enough to play on and create a crazy scenario. The choice to define zombies as an enemy seemed as an enemy seemed logical to us in view of the presence of a virus and a hospital.

Origin

The map is inspired by the look of the Mob of the Dead in Call Of Duty Black Ops 2. In the sense that our players will be in a hospital and not a prison and will be in a building with no access to the outside. The puzzles in our game are related to the "Easter Eggs" secrets, allowing to give a goal to our game other than surviving as long as possible. The idea of implementing a game master and optional quests comes from escape games, in which a voice tells us the different quests and paths to take in order to win the game.

Gameplay

The choice of adding characteristics to each of our seemed to us to be a good way to diversify the games. However, we wanted to keep the fairness between the characters, which is why we chose to keep the same base for each of them base for each of them, whether it is their equipment or their damage. Therefore, each person will be important in his own way, so we will have to ask our teammate with better attributes in order to help us in some quest. The goal is really to exploit the advantages of each one in our games.
